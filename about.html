About Me

I am currently studying at the University of Wisconsin-Madison to obtain a Geographic Information Systems (GIS) Certificate. I have taken many paths in my life, but GIS and cartography have been the culmination of my interests in programming, design, and geography.

Beginning in June 2016, I will relocate to the Atlanta area and plan to increase my skills through employment, continued study, and personal projects related to cartography and GIS.

When I’m not making maps, I am an avid traveler among other hobbies. I enjoy spending time with my dog Roxy, going to Rubik’s cube competitions, and cooking my favorite recipes.
